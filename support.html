App Information

App Name: أضواء السيارة (Car Dashboard Lights)

Platform: iOS and Android

Description: An Arabic language guide to understanding car dashboard warning lights. Browse over 50 warning lights by category, search in Arabic or English, and get detailed explanations with severity levels and recommended actions.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does the app work offline? Yes. All warning light data is stored locally. You only need an internet connection for advertisements.
  • Which languages are supported? The app interface and content are in Arabic. Search supports both Arabic and English keywords.
  • How do I reset or reinstall the app? Uninstall the app from your device and download it again from the App Store or Google Play.
  • The app crashes or freezes. What should I do? Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall. If it continues, please email us with your device model and OS version.
  • Can I request a new warning light to be added? Yes. Email us with the light description and icon, and we will consider adding it in a future update.

Reporting Bugs

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • Device model (e.g., iPhone 15, Samsung Galaxy S24)
  • OS version (e.g., iOS 17.2, Android 14)
  • App version (found in device Settings)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recording if possible
